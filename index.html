Projects

Predator Escape

The goal of this project was to provide users with a better understanding of what life is like for endangered species, such as the green sea turtle, through the use of virtual reality. Players will be living the life of a sea turtle from the moment it hatches from its egg. This educational approach allowed this project to spread awareness about endangered species and hopefully encourage others to support the cause.

Tetris

For my final digital systems laboratory project, my partner and I decided to design and implement the game of Tetris through the use of a FPGA, a VGA display and a USB keyboard. Using SystemVerilog we implemented components such as a NIOS CPU to handle the game logic, a sprite mapper for writing to frame buffers, and a double frame buffer with a VGA controller to handle drawing to the screen.

MRDC Robots

The Midwestern Robotics Design Competition (MRDC) is an annual competition held at the Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Each year, there is a new game objective that tests our ability to design, build and program a robot in one semester's time. As I transition into my senior year I look forward to next years game as its an opportunity to learn from our past mistakes and create an even better robot.
